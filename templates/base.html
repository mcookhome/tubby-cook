This website combines the APIs of both Yelp and Spotify, both commonly used websites.
Although it is not perfectly accurate, the idea was to be able to find the restaurant for a food you're craving,
with access to songs that fit the atmosphere that you're in.
Through the Yelp API, the user can search for restaurants by inputting the desired food and location.
After that, the user can then choose the desired restaurant from the results and
through the Spotify API, the user will be given a playlist of songs related to the restaurant's name.
(A Spotify account is required in order to access the playlist)
